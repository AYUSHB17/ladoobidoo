Our Story

Ladoo Bidoo is more than just a sweet — it's a feeling, a memory, a tradition. Every ladoo we make carries the warmth of a home kitchen and the pride of Maharashtrian heritage.

What started in a small home kitchen, with a love for authentic flavors and a heart full of dreams, has now become a mission to spread sweetness and smiles across every household.

Each ladoo is handcrafted with pure desi ghee, the finest quality boondi, and the perfect measure of sweetness — just like it's made for our own family. No shortcuts, no compromises.
